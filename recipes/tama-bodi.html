Tama-bodi (Nepali cuisine)

Steps to make Tama Bodi

  1. 1 heat some oil in a pan and add methi, cumin seeds, ginger garlic paste and chopped onions.
  2. 2 as the onions turn pink add tomatoes. Red chilli powder, cumin powder, boiled potatoes and bamboo shoots.
  3. 3 mix all the ingredients well and stir fry.
  4. 4 garnish with coriander leaves and your aloo tama is ready to be served.
